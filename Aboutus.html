Sayyid Hamid Senior Secndary School was established under section 12(i) of the university Act 1920. Prior to this classes were managed in the respective Faculity of the University . Two section of class IX and XI with a strength of 55 student each are introduced from the academic Session 2014-15. Studemnts in Class IX and XI are admitted through All India Admission Test.

Around 700 Student are enrolled every year in Class XI through National level Entrance Test and subsiquently same number are promoted to Class XII. The School runs Science (PCM/PCB) , Commerce And Humanities Stream in Class XI and XII. Total strength of this School including the students admitted under Self Finance Scheme comes to Around 2300.

Since its inception , The School has been a Trail-Blazer in the field of Educations. Students have performed exceptionally well in Class XII examination. Students from this portal have excelled in IIT admission competed in National Defence Academy Examination. As A feeder Institute of the Aligarh Muslim University , the School is providing quality education to student and a fillip to ensurea promoting future for the School in their University Life.
